👋🏼Hi! I’m Yussouf Ntwali.

Hi, I'm Yussouf Ntwali, a Rwandan entrepreneur passionate about education, technology and community building. I've been involved in several startups as a founder, operator and business 'guy' in Rwanda. I'm the Co-Founder & CEO of BAG, one of the fastest growing edtechs in Africa.

During the pandemic, I founded Startups in Rwanda, a community of early-stage founders and tech enthusiasts in Rwanda that promotes collaboration and ecosystem building through events and podcasts. I'm an active member of various fellowships including Kigali Global Shapers, Transcend Network, Westerwelle Young Founders Alumni, and ChangemakerXchange.

Fun fact: Before my tech career, I was a music artist and released more than a dozen songs between 2011 and 2018.
